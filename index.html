KAKOOMA

Kakooma is a puzzle game featuring two distinct modes: Plus Mode and Times Mode. Each mode has four difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert. In Plus Mode, players are given four options, where combining two options results in the third as the correct answer. In Times Mode, players must perform multiplication to find the correct answer. Each correct answer increases the score, and players have fixed time to submit each answer, making the game both challenging and fast-paced.

X-DUEL

X-Duel — A Telegram WebApp card battle game where players build a custom deck and face off against AI opponents across three lanes. Each match begins with five hand cards, each having a specific initialization time based on its level. Once a card is ready, players can instantly place it on any lane to attack or defend. The battle continues until time runs out or a player’s health reaches zero — the one with higher health at the end wins.
